About the MRC DTP Symposium

Welcome to the official site for the 2025 MRC DTP Symposium, hosted at Clare College on the 11th of November. This one-day event brings together MRC-funded PhD students to share research progress and network with peers. The symposium features keynote presentations from leading academics in the field.

If you any questions about abstracts, posters or access needs, please contact us at placeholder@mail.com. We look forward to seeing you!

Registration

Registration is straightforward and free for all MRC DTP students. Please complete the Google Form by the XYZ. The formal dinner is free of charge, with seats allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. If you miss the registration deadline but would still like to attend, please email placeholder@mail.com and we will do our best to accommodate you.

It is very important that you sign up with your @cam.ac.uk mail address .
