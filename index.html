About the MRC DTP Symposium

Welcome to the official site for the 2025 MRC DTP Symposium, hosted at the University of Cambridge on the 11th of November. This one-day event brings together MRC-funded PhD students to share research progress and network with peers The symposium features keynote presentations from leading academics in the field.

Use the navigation above to:

  • Register for the symposium and the formal dinner (free for DTP students)
  • View the Program, including keynote talks and poster sessions.
  • Get Directions to the event venues on the Cambridge campus

If you any questions about abstracts, posters or access needs, please contact us at mdtp-symposium@cam.ac.uk. We look forward to seeing you !
